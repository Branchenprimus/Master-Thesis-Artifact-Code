 This is a query that could be used to find all the cats in Wikidata. The query could be extended to find cats in a specific language or other properties.

```sparql
SELECT ?cat ?catLabel WHERE {
  ?cat wdt:P31 wd:Q146 . # instance of cat
  SERVICE wikibase:label { bd:serviceParam wikibase:language "[AUTO_LANGUAGE]" .
}
```
